## AO Weighting (All Boards)

| AO | Requirement | Weight |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AO1 | Knowledge and understanding of concepts, terms, issues | 30% |
| AO2 | Application to contexts and actions | 30% |
| AO3 | Analysis and evaluation of evidence, viewpoints, substantiated judgements | 40% |

Per paper (AQA): AO1 15%, AO2 15%, AO3 20%. Total scaled marks: 160.

## Exam Structure at a Glance

| Board | Paper 1 | Paper 2 | Duration each | Marks each |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **AQA 8100** | Active citizenship + Politics and participation | Life in modern Britain + Rights and responsibilities | 1h 45m | 80 |
| **Edexcel 1CS0** | Living together + Democracy + Law + Debates (Section D ~31 marks) | Citizenship in perspective + Power and influence (~42 marks in B) | 1h 45m | 80 |
| **OCR J270** | Citizenship in perspective | Citizenship in action | 1h 45m | 80 |

**Active citizenship (AQA):** Paper 1 Section A, 40 marks, ~15% of total GCSE. Assessed by written exam on investigation (research, action, reflection) plus others' actions.

## Active Citizenship Assessment Criteria (Exam)

Students must demonstrate in writing (AQA Paper 1 Section A style):

| Stage | What examiners credit | AO |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Research | Primary/secondary sources; methods; summary of evidence | AO1, AO3 |
| Planning | Clear aims; realistic action plan | AO2 |
| Action | Linkage between aims, research, and action taken | AO2 |
| Reflection | Success/difficulty; evaluation of usefulness | AO2, AO3 |
| Analysis | Why activity counts as citizenship action; range of valid reasons | AO2, AO3 |

**Edexcel citizenship action planning questions** use six YES/NO criteria (clear goal, spec-linked topic, impact potential, resources, concepts link, measurable success).
