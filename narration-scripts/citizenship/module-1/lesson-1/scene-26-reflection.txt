Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

So, what really makes us who we are? Hopefully by now you've realised there isn't just one answer. Your identity is built from many different pieces—your family, your interests, your culture, your experiences, your community, and the country you live in. Every person's story is different, and that's something to celebrate. Britain is made up of millions of unique identities, all contributing to one diverse society. As you continue through this course, keep thinking about your own story and how you fit into the wider picture of modern Britain.
