Scene 18 – Modern Britain
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Today, the United Kingdom is home to people with roots in almost every country in the world. This diversity is one of Britain's greatest strengths. It influences the food we eat, the music we enjoy, the sports we play, the festivals we celebrate, and even the languages we hear every day. Living in a diverse society gives us the chance to learn from people with different experiences and perspectives. Although our backgrounds may be different, those differences help create the vibrant, welcoming, and multicultural Britain that millions of people proudly call home.
