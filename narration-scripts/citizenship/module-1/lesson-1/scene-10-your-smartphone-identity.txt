Scene 10 – Your Smartphone Identity
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Now think about your smartphone for a moment. If someone unlocked it and looked at your home screen, what would they learn about you? They might notice your favourite apps, your wallpaper, the music you listen to, the games you play, or the people you message the most. No two phones are exactly alike because every person customises theirs differently. Identity works in much the same way. It's built from your interests, relationships, experiences, beliefs, and choices. Just like your home screen tells a story about you, your identity reflects the unique person you are becoming every single day.
