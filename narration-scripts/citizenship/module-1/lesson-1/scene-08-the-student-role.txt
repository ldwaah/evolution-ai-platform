Scene 8 – The Student Role
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Right now, one of your most important roles is being a student. That means far more than simply sitting in a classroom or completing homework. Being a student is about learning new ideas, developing skills, solving problems, asking questions, and becoming more confident every day. Sometimes learning feels easy, and sometimes it's challenging, but every experience helps you grow. The knowledge, resilience, and confidence you build now won't just help you pass exams—they'll help prepare you for opportunities and challenges you'll face throughout your future.
