Scene 22 – Language
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Language is another great example of cultures coming together. Listen to how young people speak in many towns and cities across the UK today. It isn't simply one version of English. Instead, language has developed through influences from Caribbean English and Patois, South Asian languages, African languages, and traditional British English. Linguists have identified one variety of this as Multicultural London English, often called MLE. It's a reminder that language changes over time, reflecting the people, communities, and cultures that shape modern Britain.
