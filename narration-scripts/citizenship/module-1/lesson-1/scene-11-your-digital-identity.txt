Scene 11 – Your Digital Identity
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

How much time do you spend online every day? Whether you're gaming, watching videos, chatting with friends, or scrolling through social media, your digital life connects you to lots of different communities. The things you choose to follow, like, comment on, or share often reveal something about your interests and personality. That's why we talk about having a digital identity. Just like in the real world, your behaviour online shapes how other people see you. Every post and every comment leaves an impression, so making positive choices online is just as important as making them face to face.
