Scene 6 – Your Family Roles
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

One of the first places our identity begins to develop is at home. Within our families, we all have different roles. You might be a son or daughter, a brother or sister, or perhaps an older sibling who helps look after younger family members. Some people even help care for parents or grandparents. These responsibilities often teach us valuable qualities such as patience, kindness, teamwork, and responsibility. Every family is different, but the role you play at home helps shape the way you see yourself and how you interact with other people every day.
