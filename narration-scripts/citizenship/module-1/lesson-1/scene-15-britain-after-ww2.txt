Scene 15 – Britain After the Second World War
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

After the Second World War, Britain faced a huge challenge. Much of the country needed rebuilding, and there simply weren't enough workers to do the job. Roads, railways, hospitals, factories, and public services all needed people. To help rebuild the country, the government encouraged people from across the Commonwealth to come and live and work in the UK. Thousands accepted the invitation, bringing valuable skills and experience. Their hard work helped rebuild Britain and left a lasting impact that can still be seen in communities across the country today.
