Scene 20 – Grime and Drill
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Think about genres like Grime and Drill. These styles of music were created here in the United Kingdom, particularly in cities such as London. Although they're proudly British, they have been influenced by many different cultures, including Caribbean, African, and British musical traditions. This shows that culture doesn't stand still. As different communities meet, they share ideas, creativity, and experiences that inspire something completely new. Modern British music is a great example of how diversity has helped shape a unique national identity that is recognised around the world.
