Scene 4 – A Big Question
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Before we look at the United Kingdom, its history, or even the map, let's begin with the most important person in today's lesson: you. Imagine someone stopped you in the street and asked, "Who are you?" How would you answer? Would you talk about your name, your family, your hobbies, your culture, or the people who matter most to you? There isn't one correct answer, because identity is made up of many different pieces. Throughout this lesson, you'll discover how those pieces fit together to create the unique person you are today.
