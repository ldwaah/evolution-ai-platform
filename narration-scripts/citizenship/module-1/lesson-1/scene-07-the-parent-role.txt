Scene 7 – The Parent Role
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

As people grow older, their identity often changes. For many adults, becoming a parent or guardian becomes one of the biggest parts of who they are. Looking after a child isn't simply another responsibility—it's a role that influences the choices they make every single day. They provide guidance, protection, encouragement, and support, often putting someone else's needs before their own. Even if you aren't a parent yourself, this shows that our identities continue to develop throughout life as our responsibilities and experiences grow.
