Scene 13 – What Is Migration?
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

Migration simply means people moving from one place to another to live. Sometimes people move to another town, while others move across the world to begin a completely new life. This has been happening for thousands of years, long before modern countries even existed. In fact, migration has played a huge role in shaping the United Kingdom. Different groups of people have brought new skills, ideas, traditions, languages, and cultures that have influenced the way Britain has grown. Without migration, the country we know today would look very different.
