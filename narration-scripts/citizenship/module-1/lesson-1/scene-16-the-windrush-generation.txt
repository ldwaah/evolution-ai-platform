Scene 16 – The Windrush Generation
Lesson: Module 1, Lesson 1 – Who Are We?

One of the most important moments in modern British history happened in 1948, when the HMT Empire Windrush arrived carrying passengers from the Caribbean. Many had answered Britain's call for workers after the war and went on to work in the NHS, public transport, factories, and other essential services. They helped rebuild the country while also bringing rich traditions, music, food, and culture. Today, we remember the Windrush Generation for its enormous contribution to Britain and recognise the important role it played in shaping the diverse country we live in today.
