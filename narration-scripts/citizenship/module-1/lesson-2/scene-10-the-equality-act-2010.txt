# Scene 10 – The Equality Act 2010

Let's begin with one of the most important laws protecting people in modern Britain: the Equality Act 2010. Before this law existed, there were lots of separate laws dealing with different types of discrimination. The Equality Act brought them together into one clear piece of legislation. Its purpose is simple, to make it illegal for people to be treated unfairly because of key parts of their identity. Whether you're applying for a job, attending school, or using public services, this law helps ensure everyone is treated fairly and equally.
