# Scene 4 – Rights vs Wants

People often confuse rights with things they simply want. Imagine you want the latest phone, expensive trainers, or a new games console. They might be nice to have, but nobody is legally required to give them to you. Those are wants. Rights are completely different. A legal right is something protected by law. It's there to protect your freedom, your safety, and your dignity. Rights aren't rewards for good behaviour, and they aren't gifts from the government. They're legal protections that apply to everyone living in the UK.
