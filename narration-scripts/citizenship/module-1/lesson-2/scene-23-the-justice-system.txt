# Scene 23 – The Justice System

So what's the purpose of the justice system? At its heart, it's about fairness. The justice system exists to protect innocent people, hold those who break the law accountable, and help keep communities safe. Judges, magistrates, juries, lawyers, and the police all have different responsibilities, but they work towards the same goal: making decisions based on evidence and applying the law fairly. While no justice system is perfect, the aim is always to make sure justice is carried out equally, regardless of who you are.
