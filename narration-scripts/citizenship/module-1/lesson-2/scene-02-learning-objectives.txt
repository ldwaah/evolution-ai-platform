# Scene 2 – Learning Objectives

Let's begin by looking at today's learning goals. By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to explain what a legal right actually is and understand how it's different from something you simply want. You'll learn about the Equality Act 2010 and the people it protects. Finally, we'll explore one of the most important principles in British democracy: the Rule of Law. You'll see why it matters and how it affects everyone, whether you're a student, a police officer, or even the Prime Minister.
