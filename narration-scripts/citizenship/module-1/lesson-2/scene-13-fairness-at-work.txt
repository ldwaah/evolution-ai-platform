# Scene 13 – Fairness at Work

Equality doesn't stop at school. It also applies in the workplace. The Equality Act helps ensure that people doing the same job are treated fairly, including receiving equal pay where the law requires it. It also requires employers to make reasonable adjustments for people with disabilities, helping remove unnecessary barriers that might stop someone from succeeding. The aim isn't to give people an advantage. It's to create a fair opportunity for everyone to do their best.
