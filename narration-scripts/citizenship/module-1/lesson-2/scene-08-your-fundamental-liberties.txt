# Scene 8 – Your Fundamental Liberties

Let's look at two important rights in a little more detail. First is the right to liberty. This means people can't simply be arrested or detained without a lawful reason. The police must act within the law. Second is the right to a fair trial. If someone is accused of committing a crime, they have the right to a fair hearing before an independent court. Decisions should always be based on evidence, not assumptions, appearance, or personal opinions. These protections help ensure that justice is fair for everyone.
