# Scene 15 – Freedom of Expression

Living in a democracy means people should be able to express their opinions, even when others disagree with them. In the UK, freedom of expression is an important right. It allows people to share their views, discuss ideas, criticise those in power, and take part in peaceful protests. These freedoms help keep democracy healthy by allowing different voices to be heard. However, like many rights, freedom of expression comes with responsibilities that protect the rights of other people too.
