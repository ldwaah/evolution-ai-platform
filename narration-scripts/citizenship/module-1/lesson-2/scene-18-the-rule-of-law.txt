# Scene 18 – The Rule of Law

One of the most important principles in the UK is called the Rule of Law. It simply means that everyone must obey the law, regardless of who they are. It doesn't matter whether someone is wealthy, famous, a police officer, or even the Prime Minister. If they break the law, they are expected to face the same legal system as everyone else. The Rule of Law helps ensure fairness, prevents abuse of power, and gives people confidence that justice should apply equally to every citizen.
