# Scene 3 – Part One: What Are Rights?

Let's start with a simple question. What exactly is a right? Is it something you earn? Is it something someone gives you? Or is it something protected by law? Before we look at the different laws that protect people in the UK, we first need to understand what rights actually are and why every citizen should know about them. Once you understand your rights, you're much better equipped to recognise when they're being respected, and when they might not be.
