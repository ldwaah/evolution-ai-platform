# Scene 1 – Welcome

Welcome back to Citizenship. In our last lesson, we explored who we are, how our identities are formed, and how migration has helped shape modern Britain. Today, we're taking the next step. We're moving from understanding ourselves to understanding the rules that protect us every day. Whether you're at school, out with friends, or walking through your local community, you have legal rights, but you also have responsibilities. By the end of this lesson, you'll understand how both work together to create a fair society.
