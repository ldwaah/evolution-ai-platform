# Scene 22 – Your Responsibilities

Rights are only one side of being a citizen. The other side is responsibility. Living in a society means recognising that our actions affect the people around us. One responsibility is following the law and helping to keep our communities safe. Another is paying taxes when we earn money, helping to fund services like the NHS, schools, roads, and emergency services. As adults, we may also be called to serve on a jury, helping make fair decisions in court. A strong society depends on people understanding both their rights and their responsibilities.
