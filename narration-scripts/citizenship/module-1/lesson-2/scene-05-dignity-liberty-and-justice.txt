# Scene 5 – Dignity, Liberty and Justice

If rights exist to protect us, what exactly are they protecting? Think of three important ideas: dignity, liberty, and justice. Dignity means every person deserves to be treated with respect, regardless of who they are. Liberty means you have the freedom to make choices and live your life without unnecessary interference. Justice means the law should treat people fairly, giving everyone the opportunity to be heard and judged on the evidence. These three ideas sit at the heart of many of the rights we enjoy today.
