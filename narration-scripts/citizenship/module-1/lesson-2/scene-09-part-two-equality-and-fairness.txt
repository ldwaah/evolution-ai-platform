# Scene 9 – Part Two: Equality and Fairness

Now that we've explored our basic rights, let's move on to another important question. What happens when someone is treated unfairly because of who they are? This is where equality laws become incredibly important. In the next section, we'll explore how the Equality Act 2010 protects people from discrimination and helps make sure everyone has the opportunity to be treated fairly at school, at work, and in everyday life. Understanding equality is an essential part of understanding citizenship.
